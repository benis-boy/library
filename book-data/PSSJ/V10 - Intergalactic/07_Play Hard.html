I can't keep my voice contained as the stinging liquid fixes my possibly broken nose and stitches up the torn skin.

Biting through the pain, I pull my hand up to my face, hoping to Arceus it's not too late - or better yet, hoping it's not even broken in the first place.

While shielding my face, the potion keeps doing its job, fixing the ligaments in my knuckles and soothing my aching wrists. It still stings like hell.

My nose is fine. Pushing a breath out my nostrils blasts the rest of the rapidly clotting blood out. Through gritted teeth, I hiss out. "Tear up my shirt and do my right shoulder next."

Lopunny and Lucario listen, and by the time my brand-new sports shirt falls to the ground and the potion runs empty, the pain eases.

Free to move my arm without hellish pain again, I reach into the medicine compartment of my backpack and get the next potion.

My fingers jitter when I'm pressing the button to apply the stinging miracle liquid to my other arm, chest, and stomach.

Lucario reaches for my hand, wrapping it within her paws. ~"Master, let me." She gently pries the bottle from me before treating the rest of the visible injuries. After pointing out the less visible parts, she treats those as well, and the second potion runs empty.

Finally, I flip to my back and stare at the sky.

Blue. With the sun past its zenith. Two Pidgey look at us from the branches of a nearby tree. A slight look of terror is visible in their expressive eyes.

Lopunny is panicking. Her voice is incoherent as she repeats her name over and over again like any pokemon.

"Well done, Lopunny."

"Lo-- Lo? Lopunny?! Lopunny!"

"Calm down first. I can't understand you like that." I look her over.

She's bloody. My blood is all over her face in tiny red droplets that seep into her sweat-soaked fur.

She takes a few deep breaths before trying again. ~"What do you mean, well done?! I--" -- "Lopunny!"

She cuts off at the end. Heh. It's been quite a while since we've been so out of sync. But from the context... "No, you didn't almost kill me. I was just in a little bit of pain. And now I just need a Sitrus Berry, and I'll be alright again."

~"Here." Salazzle already has it ready.

"Thanks." I bite down. Yuck. A smorgasbord of flavor. It's too bitter, too sweet, too spicy, and too sour. Honestly, a terrible sensation. But necessary. Everything the topical potion failed to restore, the berry will handle.

"There. I'm perfectly fine now. Just don't press on the bruises."

Lopunny, Salazzle, Lucario, and even Swadloon, using String Shot to move, then decide to ignore my plea. They press on my bruises as they all hug and cuddle me as if I just barely escaped death.

"I love you all too."
