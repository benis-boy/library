"Hey." Banette doesn't react. "Hey, Banette. Chin up, will you? We're going to rescue her, right now."

Slowly, with twitching motions, she nods. Her body then dissolves, her limbs becoming one with the shadow until just her head pokes from the ground. She stares at me, crimson eyes filled to the brim with bloody tears, and her voice croaks. ~"Will you carry me once more?"

It didn't feel nearly as painful or bizarre in my brain this time. So, with a smile on my lips, I extend my arms to invite her. "Of course."

Then she dips fully below ground and merges with my shadow.

'Carry me' must have just meant to let her travel with me. Haha... Now I feel dumb with my arms out.
